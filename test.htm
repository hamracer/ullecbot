In August, we'll have a good mix of trainees that you can add to your existing roster. Smart Falcon is the second dirt-focused character after Haru Urara, so she would be a valuable addition to your team for team trials.

At the end of the month, Mayano Top Gun, and Air Groove will release their wedding costumes. Players looking forward to these characters can start saving up now if they want to use the characters with their new costumes.

Expected Banner Schedule

Our scheduled banner releases are based on those of the Japanese version. However, we have made adjustments to align with the pacing of the Global banner releases.

With that in mind, please remember that these are only estimates. The Global version might change the release schedule to adjust expectations and keep things interesting.

Smart Falcon

Smart Falcon is a strong dirt character who will definitely see a lot of use in team trials. She and Haru Urara are the only ones with a high rating for dirt-focused tracks. Her passive skills are also well-suited for dirt, and she will undoubtedly excel in this race type.

While seemingly decent at first, Narita Brian's true value lies in her unique skill, Shadow Break, which is incredibly potent. giving her an edge on races. This is comparable to Symboli Rudolf's Behold Thine Emperor's Divine Might skill, but with a much lenient requirement for activation.

Mayano's Top Gun's strength lies in her flexibility. She can participate in Sprint, Mile, and Medium races without much difficulty and can be built to specialize in any of them. She can fill most roles on your team, making her a great flex pick.

Air Groove (Wedding) is a decent, if not exceptional, character. Her preferred race types, Medium and Long, make her difficult to recommend. There are already plenty of alternatives for those race types, so it's hard to justify spending Carats on her.

Upcoming Support Cards

Sakura Chiyono O

Sakura Chiyono O
Rarity SSR Specialty Stamina
Unique Perk Training Effectiveness and Race Bonus

Sakura Chiyono O provides decent bonuses to stamina for mile and medium-length racers, as well as pace chasers. It's a decent stamina support card if you don't have Super Creek.

Kawakami Princess

Kawakami Princess
Rarity SSR Specialty Speed
Unique Perk Speed Bonus and Initial Speed

Kawakami Princess specializes in Speed and has a good suite of flexible passives. Sadly, her support card is in the same banner as Hishi Akebono, which holds her back.

Hishi Akebono

Hishi Akebono
Rarity SSR Specialty Guts
Unique Perk Mood Effect and Specialty Priority

Hishi Akebono's support card is held back mainly because it specializes in Guts. Since Guts provides little value as a stat, this effectively neuters her viability in the current meta.
